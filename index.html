About The Event

UNEC (UPN National English Competition) is an annual national-scale English competition organized by the Language Center and the Global Language Club (GLC) student activity unit at UPN "Veteran" Jawa Timur. This competition is intended for high school and university students across Indonesia to showcase their talents.

Competition Categories

Speech
